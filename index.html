About Me

Hi, I'm Abhishek Mevada — a passionate Web Developer and Data Science & Analytics enthusiast with a strong interest in Machine Learning. I love building modern, user-friendly web applications that not only look great but also deliver meaningful functionality.

My journey in technology began with a curiosity to understand how things work behind the scenes — from web interactions to the power of data in shaping decisions. Over time, I’ve honed my skills in front-end and back-end development, data visualization, and predictive analytics, allowing me to bridge the gap between data and design.

I thrive on solving complex problems, whether it’s optimizing a website’s performance or uncovering insights from large datasets. I enjoy working on projects that challenge me to learn, innovate, and create value through clean code and data-driven thinking.

When I’m not coding or exploring new tools, you’ll likely find me learning about emerging technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or diving into a good book about AI and innovation.

My goal is to continue growing as a developer and data scientist — combining creativity, logic, and data to build intelligent solutions that make a real-world impact.
